## 👋 About Me

I'm **Raj**, Founder of **Blue Horizon** — a software studio building intelligent, production-grade platforms. I work across the full stack: **React/Vite** front ends, **Node.js/Express + MongoDB** backends, real-time systems with **Stream Video/Chat**, and AI integrations powered by **Claude, Gemini, and Groq**.

I care about clean architecture, polished UI/UX, and shipping things that feel like real products — not prototypes.

- 🔭 Currently building **InterVue** — an AI-powered technical interview platform
- 🧠 Also maintaining **ClassMark** (attendance management) and **TruthLens AI** (RAG fact-checking)
- 🌱 Exploring system design and scalable backend architecture
- 📫 Reach me at **rajposture16@gmail.com**
